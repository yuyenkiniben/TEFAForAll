About Us

Help Cameroonians to get the best type of education to develop and shape their society

Our Mission:
  1. OUR MAIN MISSION IS TO REACH OUT TO EVERY CAMEROONIAN WHETHER RICH OR POOR, YOUNG OR OLD WHO TASTE FOR KNOWLEDGE.
  2. TO HELP CAMEROONIANS WHO CAN’T GO TO SCHOOL, e.g. THE DISABLED, HAWKERS, BUSINESS MEN AND WOMEN ETC. WHO DO NOT HAVE TIME TO GO AND SIT IN A CLASS ROOM BUT ARE WILLING TO OBTAIN A GCE , A-LEVEL , BAC, PROBATOIRE ETC. CERTIFICATES (BY HELPING THEM REGISTER AS EXTERNAL STUDENTS IN OTHER SCHOOLS). ALSO THIS WILL HELP DOES WHO GO TO EVENING SCHOOLS.
  3. THIS WILL GIVE A BETTER APPROACH OF REASONING TO MOST CAMEROONIANS SINCE MOST OF THEM RELY ON WHAT TEACHERS GIVE THEM, IT WILL HELP THEM DO EXTRA RESEARCH USING MODERN COMMUNICTION DEVICES THAT SURROUNDS THEM.
Our story

Founded by two Cameroonians WIRNDZEREM BLAISE FONYUY and YUYEN KINIBEN TANLAKA in 2016. And TEFA is an online learning platform based on the MOOC architecture. It offers the best and high quality standard courses by the best Schools in the nation given by the best teachers that we can have in Cameroon. Our Learning Community All Cameroons starting from the Secondary level right up to the university level. Interested to learn any course based on the Cameroonian standards. Our Difference Our main difference is that we help our community or our students archive Cameroonian based certificates which is very different from other MOOC platforms. We don’t want knowledge that can’t be applied to our mother land Cameroon. Moreover we are completely free with what we do.
